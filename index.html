ELECTRONIC PRODuCER

CHICAGO/DETROIT


Everett Reid is a drummer and electronic producer from the South Side of Chicago. Freshly moved to Detroit after having graduated from University of Michigan’s School of Music, he’s humbled himself to the city in developing his means of musical service to the local jazz and electronic scenes. His wish is to help build and stimulate Detroit’s vibrantly active arts culture, and to serve individuals in the city and abroad with a sound that both empowers and inspires them to experience their best self.
